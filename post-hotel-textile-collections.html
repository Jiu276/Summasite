In the competitive world of hospitality, every detail matters. The softness of bed linens, the weight of dining napkins, the plushness of towels - these tactile experiences shape guest perceptions and drive satisfaction. Richard Haworth understands this intimately, offering comprehensive hotel textile collections that elevate the guest experience from check-in to checkout.

For over 125 years, Richard Haworth has been the trusted partner for luxury hotels, boutique accommodations, and fine dining establishments across the UK. Their complete textile solutions ensure consistency, quality, and that intangible sense of luxury that transforms a good stay into an unforgettable experience.

A Legacy of Quality and Service

Founded in 1898, Richard Haworth has witnessed the evolution of the hospitality industry firsthand. What began as a family business supplying linens to local establishments has grown into one of the UK's premier providers of comprehensive hotel textile solutions.

"Quality textiles aren't just about thread count - they're about creating moments of comfort that guests remember long after they've checked out." - The philosophy behind Richard Haworth

What sets Richard Haworth apart is their holistic approach. They don't just supply textiles - they partner with hospitality businesses to create cohesive environments where every textile element works in harmony to deliver the desired guest experience.

Bedroom Textiles: Where Comfort Begins

A guest's bedroom experience can make or break their entire stay. Richard Haworth's bedroom collections feature:

  • Premium Bed Linens: From classic percale to luxurious sateen, sheets in thread counts ranging from 200 to 1000TC
  • Duvet Options: Multiple tog ratings and filling choices including down, feather, and hypoallergenic alternatives
  • Pillow Variety: Soft, medium, and firm options to accommodate every sleeping preference
  • Mattress Protection: High-quality protectors that extend mattress life while maintaining comfort
  • Decorative Accents: Throws, cushions, and bedspreads that add visual appeal and warmth

Every item is carefully sourced and tested to ensure it can withstand the rigors of commercial laundering while maintaining its luxurious feel wash after wash.

Bathroom Textiles: Spa-Like Comfort

The bathroom experience should feel like a personal spa retreat. Richard Haworth offers bathroom textiles in various weights and qualities:

Towel Collections: From lightweight and quick-drying to plush and absorbent, their towel ranges cater to different hotel positioning - from budget-friendly options for high-turnover properties to ultra-luxurious 700GSM towels for five-star establishments.

Bath Robes: Available in various styles including waffle, terry, and velour, with options for embroidered branding to create a cohesive brand experience.

Bath Mats: Non-slip, highly absorbent mats that combine safety with comfort, available in standard and custom sizes.

Dining Textiles: Setting the Stage

In restaurants and dining spaces, textiles play a crucial role in creating ambiance. Richard Haworth's dining collections include:

Table Linens: Classic white tablecloths remain timeless, but Richard Haworth also offers colored and patterned options to match your restaurant's aesthetic. Stain-resistant treatments ensure linens maintain their pristine appearance.

Napkins: Available in various sizes and qualities, from cocktail napkins to large dinner napkins, all designed to withstand frequent washing while maintaining their crisp fold.

Table Runners & Placemats: Add visual interest and protect table surfaces while offering easier maintenance than full tablecloths.

Flexible Solutions for Every Business Model

Richard Haworth understands that different businesses have different needs. They offer both purchase and rental options, allowing hotels to choose the model that best fits their operational and financial requirements.

Rental Services: Ideal for managing working capital and ensuring consistent quality through professional laundering services. Rental agreements include regular replacement of worn items, maintaining consistent quality standards.

Direct Purchase: For properties with in-house laundry facilities or those preferring to own their inventory, direct purchase offers complete control over textile assets.
