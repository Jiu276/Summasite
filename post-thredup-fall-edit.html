Building Your Fall Capsule Wardrobe

The beauty of ThredUP's Fall Edit lies in its ability to help you create a cohesive wardrobe without the premium price tag. Here's how to build a versatile fall capsule:

Start with Neutrals: Invest in quality basics in camel, cream, black, and navy. These timeless pieces from ThredUP form the foundation that everything else builds upon.

Add Statement Pieces: With the money saved on basics, splurge on that designer coat you've been eyeing or those perfect vintage boots. ThredUP makes luxury accessible.

Embrace Layering: Fall fashion is all about layers. Mix textures and weights to create depth in your outfits - think silk camisoles under cashmere sweaters or denim jackets over floral dresses.

Fashion with a Conscience

Choosing ThredUP isn't just about finding great deals - it's about participating in a fashion revolution. The fashion industry is one of the world's largest polluters, but by extending the life of existing garments, we can significantly reduce waste and environmental impact.

Every secondhand purchase saves thousands of gallons of water, reduces carbon emissions, and prevents perfectly good clothing from ending up in landfills. When you shop ThredUP's Fall Edit, you're not just updating your wardrobe - you're voting for a more sustainable future.

Clean Out Your Closet, Earn Cash

ThredUP isn't just for buying - it's also the easiest way to declutter your closet and earn money from clothes you no longer wear. Simply order a Clean Out Kit, fill it with items you're ready to part with, and send it back for free.

ThredUP handles the photography, pricing, and shipping - you just collect the earnings. It's the perfect circular fashion economy: make room for new-to-you pieces while passing along items that deserve another life.
