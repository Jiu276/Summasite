About Summasite

Empowering conscious consumers to make sustainable choices that benefit both their lives and our planet.

Our Mission

At Summasite, we believe that living sustainably shouldn't mean compromising on quality, style, or convenience. Our mission is to bridge the gap between environmental consciousness and modern living by providing honest, detailed reviews of eco-friendly products and actionable guidance for a more sustainable lifestyle.

Founded in 2022, we've grown from a passion project into a trusted resource for thousands of eco-conscious consumers worldwide. Our team of environmental experts, product testers, and sustainability advocates works tirelessly to research, test, and review products that align with our values of environmental responsibility and quality excellence.

Community First

Education

We believe in empowering consumers with knowledge to make informed sustainable choices.

Our Review Process

Research & Selection

We carefully research and select products based on environmental certifications, ingredient transparency, company ethics, and reader requests.

Comprehensive Testing

Each product undergoes extensive real-world testing for at least 30 days, evaluating performance, durability, and user experience.

Impact Assessment

We analyze the environmental impact, from production and packaging to disposal and recyclability, ensuring genuine sustainability.

Honest Review

Our detailed reviews highlight both pros and cons, helping you make informed decisions based on transparent, unbiased information.

Certifications We Look For

USDA Organic

Products made with at least 95% organic ingredients, certified by the United States Department of Agriculture.

GOTS Certified

Global Organic Textile Standard ensures organic fibers and environmental and social standards throughout the supply chain.

Fair Trade

Ensures fair wages, safe working conditions, and community development for farmers and workers worldwide.

Cradle to Cradle

Products designed for circularity, with safe ingredients, renewable energy, and responsible water use.

FSC Certified

Forest Stewardship Council certification ensures responsible forest management and sustainable sourcing.

Leaping Bunny

The gold standard for cruelty-free certification, ensuring no animal testing throughout the supply chain.
