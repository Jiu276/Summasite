Care and Longevity

To ensure your Beach Riot pieces maintain their vibrant colors and perfect fit, proper care is essential:

  • Rinse immediately after swimming in chlorinated or salt water
  • Hand wash in cold water with gentle detergent
  • Avoid wringing; gently squeeze out excess water
  • Lay flat to dry away from direct sunlight
  • Store in a cool, dry place between wears
