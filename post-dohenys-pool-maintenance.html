Choosing the Right Pool Cleaner

Robotic Cleaners: Independent units with built-in filtration, ideal for pools with minimal debris and those seeking energy efficiency.
Suction-Side Cleaners: Connect to your pool's filtration system, perfect for pools with fine debris and sand.
Pressure-Side Cleaners: Use water pressure to collect debris, excellent for pools with large debris like leaves and acorns.
