Styling Your Eby Essentials

Styling Tips

Customer Care and Longevity

To maintain the quality and comfort of your Eby pieces, proper care is essential:

  • Machine wash cold on gentle cycle
  • Use mild, fragrance-free detergent
  • Avoid fabric softeners which can affect moisture-wicking properties
  • Air dry or tumble dry on low heat
  • Store in a cool, dry place
