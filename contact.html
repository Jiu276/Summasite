Frequently Asked Questions

How do you select products to review?

We select products based on several criteria: environmental certifications, ingredient transparency, company ethics, reader requests, and market availability. We prioritize products that offer genuine sustainable alternatives to conventional options.

Do you accept products from companies for review?

Yes, we accept product samples from companies, but we maintain complete editorial independence. All products undergo our standard 30-day testing process, and we always disclose when products are provided for review. Our opinions remain honest and unbiased.

Can I suggest a product for review?

Absolutely! We love hearing from our readers about products they'd like us to test. Use our contact form with the "Product Suggestion" subject, and tell us about the product and why you think it would be valuable for our community to know about.

Do you offer consulting services?

We offer limited consulting services for sustainability-focused businesses and brands. Please contact us through our partnerships email to discuss your specific needs and how we might be able to help.

How can I support Summasite?

The best way to support us is by engaging with our content, sharing our reviews with friends, and subscribing to our newsletter. We also participate in affiliate programs, so when you purchase products through our links, we earn a small commission at no cost to you.

Can I write for Summasite?

We occasionally accept guest contributions from sustainability experts and passionate eco-advocates. Please send us your topic ideas, writing samples, and expertise background through our contact form for consideration.
